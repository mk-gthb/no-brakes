The Platform

Why no brakes? Because we're on the interstate baby.

01

All Campus Functions

Bring back the open parties everyone's been missing by putting money back in the hands of orgs that program for the people!

02

Frosh & Underclassmen Pathways

Dedicated cabinet position for underclassmen engagement focused on making The Row, Greek, and other spaces more accessible to all.

03

Row Programming

The Row Programming Fund was 40% smaller this year than in 2023, we want to expand its scope, and have the administrative backing to execute.

04

Nerd Nation

We broke records in student engagement this year, and we will continue to build a culture that celebrates the union of the nerd and the athlete. Pres and Posts worth showing up for!

05

Free Speech & Protest

Finding ways to support and encourage free speech and protest across all spaces and all times. We refuse to be apolitical.

06

Transparency

You should be able to track every dollar we spend and every grant we approve. See how we spend every hour on a new ASSU tracking portal.

07

Housing Reform

Investigate the CRL process, protect co-ops, theme houses, fix OCS, and fight for the housing process transparency you deserve.

08

Coterms, Transfers, Internationals

Dedicated spaces for affinity, accompanied by programming to support transitional periods in a student's life.

09

Event Planning 2.0

Figure out what's going on with the room booking situation, work orders, permits and licenses. Workshop a simple, intuitive process to plan an event.

10

Student-Designed Merch

You loved the Big Game shirt drops, and we'll do limited drops of free student designed merch every month—and we'll only print the stuff you want.
